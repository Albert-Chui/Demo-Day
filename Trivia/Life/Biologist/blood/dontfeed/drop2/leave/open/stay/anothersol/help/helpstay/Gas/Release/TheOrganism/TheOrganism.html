The Creature

The organism was first discovered at a crash site from a meteor. The new species was classified as aliens. It is currently kept away from the public view, in Area 51. When I found out about it, I was fascinated that extraterrestrial life exists out there. I knew I had to learn more about it. But, another part of me feared it. We don't know where it really came from, other than space. We don't know how intelligent it might be or if it could be hostile. I guess, I learned it was hostile in the end.
